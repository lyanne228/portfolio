Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

  1. Click and drag on the picture or use the left/right/up/down arrow keys to shift your view
  2. Click on the left/right side of the picture to brighten/darken the background blue
  3. The manipulation of the picture might get strange after several successive arrow key presses;
    Use mouse dragging to shift view or return to the original position to fix
  4. Use 'c' or the 'Return to Default Position' button to return to the original position
  5. Use 'v' or the 'Starfish Animation Toggle' button to start/stop starfish animation
